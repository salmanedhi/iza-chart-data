This graph compares the most frequent daily users of social media by minutes ( y axis ) with age groups ( x axis ) . 45-54 year olds spend the most amount of time on social media out of the 5 age groups , with an average of 150 minutes . 15-24 year olds spend the second most amount of time on social media , with an average of 124 minutes . 55-64 year olds spend the third most amount of time on social media , with an average of 130 minutes . 25-34 year olds spend the fourth most amount of time on social media , with an average of 74 minutes . 35-44 year olds spend the least amount of time on social media , with an average of 72 minutes .
