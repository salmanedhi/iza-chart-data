This chart shows the least frequent daily users of social media , sorted into 9 year age groups and plotted by minutes . Those aged 15-24 spent approx . 125 minutes on social media per day ; 25-34 approx . 75 minutes and 35-44 approx . 70 minutes . The most active age group on social media per day is those aged 45-54 , spending 150 minutes per day ; and finally those aged 55-64 spent approx . 110 minutes per day .
