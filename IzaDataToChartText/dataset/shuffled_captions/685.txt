This chart shows how the minority representation of diversity in the Libyan parliament has changed over the years . \n It shows that between 1990 - 1994 there was a 0.4 % diversity which increased gradually over the years . Between 1995 - 1999 the diversity was at 2 % , 2000 - 2004 it was 7.4 % , 2005 - 2009 it was 10 % , 2010 - 2014 it was 12.6 % and between 2015 - 2019 diversity was at its highest at 14 % .
