The number of fatal injuries at Pula Steel factory between 2012 and 2016 is demonstrated here . It shows there to be little change over time , with 27 in 2012 and 25 in 2016 . There was however an increase to 32 injuries between 2012 and 2013 and a steady decrease to 22 in 2015 . This decrease started to reverse in 2016 .
