This chart shows the percentage of minority representation in the Libyan parliament during different 5 - year periods between 1990 and 2019 . \n It shows a consistent increase over the years , starting with less than 1 % in 1990 - 1994 . It increases to 2 % in 1995 - 1999 , over 7 % in 2000 - 2004 , 10 % 2005 - 2009 , over 12 % in 2010 - 2014 , and finally 14 % in 2015 - 2019 .
