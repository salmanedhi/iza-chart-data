This chart shows the percentage of minority representation within the Libyan parliament . \n From the chart we see that during the years of 1990 - 1994 approximately 0.4 % of the parliament were represented by minorities . \n During the years 1995 - 1999 this rose to 2 % , and continued to increase to 7.8 % during 2000 - 2004 , increasing to 10 % during 2005 - 2009 , increasing to 12.8 % during 2010 - 2014 and increasing ti 14 % during 2015 - 2019 .
