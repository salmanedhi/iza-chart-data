This chart shows how much money is spend (in millions of dollars) on higher education in different continents . By far , Asia spends the most on higher education with over 300 million dollar spent in 2010 , followed by North America at 200 million and Europe at 100 million . Africa spent the least at 50 million . This could be due to the economic situation in each respective continent, and also due to societal norms and values: in Asia , there is a lot of emphasis placed on the value of having a good education, which explains why more is spent on higher education in Asia than any other continent.
