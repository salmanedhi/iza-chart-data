This graph compares the least frequent users of social media in minutes and their respected age group . The least frequent users are in the age groups 25-34 and 35-44 , with both of their minutes being around 75 minutes . This value is almost half of the social media minutes in the 45-54 age group ( 150 minutes ) . The age groups for 15-24 and 55-64 had minutes of around 122 and 110 minutes respectively .
