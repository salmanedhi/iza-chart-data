This graph displays the least explored glaciers by number of expeditions (y axis) , with the glacier name on the x axis . Tasman is the least explored , with 2 expeditions . Volta is the second ( 4 expeditions ) , Olav is the third ( 5 expeditions ) , Zvezda is the fourth ( 6 expeditions ) and Akkem is the fifth ( 9 expeditions ) . The sixth ( most explored of the 6 glaciers ) is Mefonna ( 13 expeditions ) .
