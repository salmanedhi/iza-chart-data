This graph the percentage of the lack of diversity in the Libyan parliament : minority representation in consecutive 4 year blocks . From first glance , it is evident that the percentage of minority inclusion is always increasing . The biggest percentage increase is 2 % - 7.5 % in the years ( 1995 - 1999 ) - ( 2000 - 2004 ) . From 2004 to 2005 and 2009 to 2010 there is an almost identical percentage increase of around 2.2 % . Over the course of the years the percentage increases from 0.2 % to 14 % .
