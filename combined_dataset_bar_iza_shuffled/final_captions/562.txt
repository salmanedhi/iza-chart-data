The chart shows the Median salary of Women in Najaf . In 2000 the median salary was approximately 22 k . Over the next 5 years there was a healthy increase to the median salary of 18 % however over the next ten years ( 2005 - 2015 ) this decreased . From 2005 to 2010 there was a 27 % decrease in median salary and a further 16 % decrease to 2015 .
